Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

Specifusion Investments (PTY) LTD operates the Goat Manager: Herd Tracker mobile app. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection & Permissions

  • Authentication: We use Google OAuth to verify your identity. We only access your email and name to create your account.
  • Camera & Files: Access is required only to allow you to take and upload photos of your livestock.
  • Google Drive: Access is requested solely for optional database backups controlled by the user.
2. Google API Services Compliance.

Goat Manager's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Cloud Storage

Your records are synced with Supabase to ensure cross-device access. Images are stored in secure Supabase Storage buckets.

4. Account Deletion

Users may delete their account and all associated data at any time via the in-app Settings menu.
