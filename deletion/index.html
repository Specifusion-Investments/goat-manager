Account and Data Deletion Request

If you no longer have the Goat Manager: Herd Tracker application installed and wish to permanently delete your account and all associated farm records from our cloud servers, please follow these steps:

Deletion Steps

  • Send an email to dev@specifusion.co.za from the email address associated with your Google Sign-In account.
  • Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Goat Manager".

We will verify your request and permanently wipe your data from our Supabase servers within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Note: If you still have the app installed, you can delete your data instantly by navigating to Settings > Delete Account inside the app.
